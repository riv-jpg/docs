A Stubly account is your passport to everything the game has to offer. It ties your progress, purchases, and player stats to your identity so you can pick up exactly where you left off whether you switch from your phone to your PC or back again. Creating an account is free and takes less than two minutes.

## Create an Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Stubly Baseball">
    Launch the game on any device. The title screen loads with two options: **Sign In** and **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Sign Up">
    Tap or click **Sign Up** to open the account creation form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your display name">
    Choose a display name that other players will see. It must be **3–20 characters** long, use only **letters, numbers, and underscores**, and be **unique** across all Stubly players. The game checks availability in real time as you type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and password">
    Provide a valid email address and create a strong password. Your password must be at least eight characters and include a mix of letters and numbers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Create Account">
    Tap **Create Account**. Stubly sends a verification email to the address you provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Open the verification email and tap the **Confirm Email** link inside. Return to the game — it detects the verification automatically and logs you in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first player">
    On your first login, the game prompts you to create your first player. Choose a name, customize your appearance, and select your primary position on the field. You can adjust these at any time in the Player Editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign-In Options

You can sign in using any of the following methods:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email & Password">
    Enter the email address and password you registered with, then tap **Sign In**. Enable **Remember Me** to stay signed in on your personal device.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Continue with Google">
    Tap **Continue with Google** and select your Google account from the picker. Stubly creates or links your account automatically — no separate password needed.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Continue with Apple">
    Tap **Continue with Apple** and authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password. You can choose to hide your email using Apple's relay service.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Account Security

Keeping your account secure protects your progress and any tokens or items you have earned.

<Accordion title="Enable 2-Step Verification">
  Navigate to **Settings → Account → 2-Step Verification** and tap **Enable**. Each time you sign in on a new device, Stubly sends a one-time code to your email or authenticator app. Enter the code to complete the sign-in.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Recover a Forgotten Password">
  On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password** below the password field. Enter your registered email address and tap **Send Reset Link**. Check your inbox for the reset email and follow the link to create a new password. The reset link expires after 30 minutes.
</Accordion>

<Tip>
  Use a password manager to generate and store a strong, unique password for your Stubly account. Avoid reusing passwords from other services.
</Tip>

## Guest Mode

If you want to try the game before creating an account, you can launch Stubly Baseball as a guest. Guest mode gives you access to **Story Mode** and **Batting Cages** right away.

<Note>
  Guest mode has important limitations. Your progress, stats, and any items you collect are **not saved** when you close the game. Online multiplayer, leaderboards, and shop purchases are unavailable to guests. Create a free account to unlock the full game and preserve everything you earn.
</Note>

To switch from guest mode to a full account at any time, open **Settings → Account → Create Account** and complete the sign-up flow. Any progress from your current guest session is carried over automatically if you convert before closing the game.
