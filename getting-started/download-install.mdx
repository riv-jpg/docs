## Install Instructions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS">
    <Tip>Connect to Wi-Fi before downloading to avoid mobile data charges.</Tip>

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the App Store">
        On your iPhone, tap the **App Store** icon on your home screen.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Search for Stubly Baseball">
        Tap the **Search** tab at the bottom, then type **Stubly Baseball** in the search bar and tap **Search**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Download the app">
        Tap the **Get** button next to the Stubly Baseball listing, then authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password to confirm the download.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait for the install to finish">
        The app icon appears on your home screen. Wait for the progress ring to complete before opening the game.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch Stubly Baseball">
        Tap the **Stubly Baseball** icon to open the game. The launcher checks for any day-one updates and applies them automatically before taking you to the title screen.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    <Tip>Connect to Wi-Fi before downloading to avoid mobile data charges.</Tip>

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Google Play">
        Tap the **Google Play** icon in your app drawer or on your home screen.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Search for Stubly Baseball">
        Tap the **Search** bar at the top, type **Stubly Baseball**, and tap the search result that matches.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install the app">
        Tap the **Install** button. Accept any permission prompts that appear. The download begins immediately.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait for the install to finish">
        A progress bar shows the download and install status. Do not close Google Play until the bar completes.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch Stubly Baseball">
        Tap **Open** in Google Play, or find the **Stubly Baseball** icon in your app drawer. The game downloads the latest patch automatically on first launch.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="PC — Windows">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit the Stubly Baseball website">
        Open your browser and go to **stublybaseball.com/download**. Click the **Download for Windows** button.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Run the installer">
        Open the downloaded **StublyBaseball_Setup.exe** file. If Windows Defender SmartScreen appears, click **More info** → **Run anyway** to proceed.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose an install location">
        Accept the default install path or choose a custom folder with at least 4 GB of free space. Click **Install**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait for installation to complete">
        The installer extracts all game files. This typically takes one to three minutes depending on your drive speed.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch Stubly Baseball">
        Click **Finish** to close the installer, then double-click the **Stubly Baseball** desktop shortcut. The launcher applies any available updates before loading the main menu.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="PC — Mac">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit the Stubly Baseball website">
        Open your browser and go to **stublybaseball.com/download**. Click the **Download for Mac** button. The **.dmg** file begins downloading.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open the disk image">
        Once the download finishes, double-click the **StublyBaseball.dmg** file in your Downloads folder to mount it.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Drag the app to Applications">
        In the window that appears, drag the **Stubly Baseball** icon into your **Applications** folder shortcut.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch Stubly Baseball">
        Open your **Applications** folder, then double-click **Stubly Baseball**. On first launch, macOS may ask you to confirm opening an app from the internet — click **Open** to continue.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Allow the launcher to update">
        The launcher checks for updates and downloads them automatically. Once the progress bar completes, the game loads to the title screen.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## After Installing

Once the initial install finishes, Stubly Baseball updates itself automatically every time you launch it. You do not need to revisit the App Store, Google Play, or the website to stay on the latest version.

<Note>
  Stubly Baseball requires an internet connection for initial setup and all online features, including multiplayer and shop purchases. After your first login, **Story Mode** and **Batting Cages** work fully offline.
</Note>

<Accordion title="Troubleshooting: Installation Errors">
  **Not enough storage** — Free up space on your device and try again. Clear app caches or uninstall unused apps if needed.

  **Download stalls on mobile** — Toggle airplane mode on and off to reset your connection, then resume the download.

  **macOS "app is damaged" warning** — Open **System Settings → Privacy & Security**, scroll to the Security section, and click **Open Anyway** next to the Stubly Baseball entry.

  **Windows installer blocked by antivirus** — Add the installer file to your antivirus allowlist, or temporarily disable real-time protection while installing.
</Accordion>
