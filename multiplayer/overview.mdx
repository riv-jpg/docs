Stubly Baseball's online multiplayer puts you head-to-head against real players from around the world in fully real-time 1v1 matches. Whether you're looking for a casual warm-up, a serious climb up the competitive ladder, or a friendly showdown with a specific opponent, three distinct online modes have you covered — each with its own matchmaking pool, rewards, and ruleset.

<Note>
  All online modes require a **Stubly account** and an active **internet connection**. You can create or sign in to your Stubly account from the main menu under **Profile → Sign In**.
</Note>

## Online Modes

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Quick Play" icon="bolt" href="/multiplayer/matchmaking">
    Jump into casual, unranked matches with the fastest matchmaking available. Earn tokens based on your in-game performance — no rank on the line.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ranked Play" icon="trophy" href="/multiplayer/ranked-play">
    Compete in the tiered ranked ladder — Bronze through Legend — and earn or lose Rank Points based on match outcomes and performance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Private Matches" icon="lock" href="/multiplayer/private-matches">
    Invite a friend and configure your own match with custom innings, stadiums, and difficulty settings. Both players earn a flat 30 tokens per match.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What to Expect Online

Every online match in Stubly Baseball is fully synchronous — both you and your opponent pitch, bat, and field in real time with server-side sync keeping everything consistent. Lag compensation is built in, so if your connection drops momentarily, the match pauses for up to **15 seconds** before auto-resolving. Here's a quick breakdown of what to expect in each mode:

| Mode | Avg. Matchmaking Time | Rewards |
|---|---|---|
| Quick Play | 10–30 seconds | Tokens (performance-based) |
| Ranked Play | Up to 60 seconds at higher tiers | Rank Points (RP) |
| Private Matches | Instant (invite-only) | Flat 30 tokens per player |

## Fair Play

Stubly Baseball is built for competitive integrity. Keep the following fair-play rules in mind before you queue:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Disconnections Count" icon="wifi-slash" href="/multiplayer/ranked-play">
    Leaving or disconnecting from a **Ranked** match counts as a loss and deducts **30 RP** from your total. Stay in the game until the final out.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sportsmanship System" icon="flag" href="/multiplayer/overview">
    Stubly tracks in-game reports from other players. Repeated violations can result in **matchmaking restrictions** that limit your access to online modes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gear Caps in Ranked" icon="shield-halved" href="/multiplayer/ranked-play">
    Gear stat bonuses are capped at **+5 per stat** in Ranked matches to keep competition skill-based. Your base player stats from upgrades are always uncapped.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Account Required" icon="circle-user" href="/multiplayer/overview">
    All online modes require a verified Stubly account. Guest accounts cannot access Quick Play, Ranked, or Private Matches.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  New to online play? Start with a few **Quick Play** games to calibrate your matchmaking rating before jumping into Ranked. Your first 5 Quick Play games place you in a dedicated calibration pool against similarly new players.
</Tip>
