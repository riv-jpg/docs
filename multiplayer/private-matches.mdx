Private Matches let you challenge a specific friend to a 1v1 game entirely outside of the Quick Play and Ranked pools. No matchmaking queue, no strangers — just you, a friend, and a fully configurable ruleset. Private Matches don't award Rank Points or performance-based token bonuses, but both players earn a flat **30 tokens** per match regardless of the result.

<Tip>
  Private Matches are a great way to practice with a friend before heading into Ranked Play. Turn off gear bonuses in the match settings to drill pure mechanics, or crank the difficulty to Simulation for a serious challenge.
</Tip>

## Creating and Joining a Room

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Private Match">
    From the main menu, swipe to the **Quick Play** card and tap **Private Match** (the lock icon in the bottom-right corner of the card).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your room">
    Tap **Create Room**. Stubly instantly generates a unique **6-character Room Code** displayed at the top of your lobby screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the Room Code">
    Send the Room Code to your friend however you like — message, voice chat, or in person. Your friend opens the same **Private Match** screen and taps **Join Room**, then enters the code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure match settings">
    Once both players appear in the lobby, the **host** (room creator) can adjust all match settings. The guest sees all changes in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the match">
    Tap **Start Match** to launch the game. The guest has **10 seconds** to ready up. If the guest doesn't ready in time, the host can start anyway or wait and try again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Match Settings

Customize every aspect of your Private Match from the lobby screen:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Game Format">
    | Setting | Options |
    |---|---|
    | **Innings** | 3, 5, 7, or 9 |
    | **Difficulty** | Normal or Simulation |
    | **Gear Bonuses** | On or Off |

    **Simulation mode** removes all aim assists and tightens timing windows for pitching and batting — it's the closest experience to real baseball mechanics the game offers.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Stadium">
    Choose from **8 available stadiums**, each with an independent **day/night toggle**. Stadium selection is available to the host only; the guest can suggest but not override.

    Stadiums range from compact minor-league parks with short foul lines to cavernous outdoor venues where only the cleanest contact goes over the wall. Experiment with different environments to keep matches interesting.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Rewards">
    | Outcome | Tokens Awarded |
    |---|---|
    | Win | 30 tokens (flat) |
    | Loss | 30 tokens (flat) |
    | Disconnect / forfeit | 30 tokens (flat) |

    Token rewards in Private Matches are always flat — performance and result don't affect the payout. Rank Points are **never** wagered or awarded in Private Matches.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Rejoining After a Disconnection

If your connection drops mid-match, don't close the app:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Return to the Private Match screen">
    Open Stubly and navigate back to **Quick Play → Private Match**. A **Rejoin** banner appears at the top if an active room is still waiting for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'Rejoin'">
    Tap the **Rejoin** button, or manually enter your original Room Code under **Join Room**. You'll drop back into the match at the exact moment it paused.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rejoin within 2 minutes">
    You have a **2-minute window** to reconnect. If neither player rejoins within that window, the match is automatically cancelled and both players still receive the flat **30 token** reward.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  The 2-minute rejoin window applies to both players. If the **host** disconnects and doesn't return in time, the room is permanently closed — the guest cannot keep it open alone. Both players should rejoin as quickly as possible to resume the game.
</Warning>
