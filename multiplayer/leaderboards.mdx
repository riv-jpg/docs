Stubly Baseball's leaderboards put your performance in perspective — you can see exactly where you stand among every player on the planet, filter by your region, and even scope the view to just your friends list. Whether you're chasing the top 100 or just trying to climb past a rival, the leaderboards give you a real-time snapshot of who's dominating the diamond.

## Accessing Leaderboards

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Main Menu">
    Launch Stubly Baseball and return to the Main Menu from any screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the Trophy Icon">
    Tap the **Trophy icon** in the top navigation bar to open the Leaderboards screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Categories">
    Swipe left and right across the leaderboard panels to switch between categories, or tap a category name in the tab bar at the top of the screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Leaderboard Categories

All six leaderboard categories track your performance across the current season. Your position resets at the start of each new season, so every competitive cycle is a fresh start.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Ranked Points" icon="trophy">
    Your overall competitive standing, measured by total RP earned this season. This is the primary category used for end-of-season rewards.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Win Rate" icon="chart-line">
    The percentage of Quick Play and Ranked games you've won. A minimum of 20 completed games is required to appear on this leaderboard.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Home Runs" icon="baseball">
    Total home runs you've hit across all game modes this season — Quick Play, Ranked, and Story Mode all count.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Batting Average" icon="chart-bar">
    Calculated from all your at-bats in Quick Play and Ranked matches. Consistency over the full season is what moves the needle here.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Strikeouts (Pitcher)" icon="wind">
    Total strikeouts you've recorded while pitching in Quick Play and Ranked games. Dominate the mound to climb this category.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Defensive Rating" icon="shield">
    A composite score built from catches made, errors prevented, and accurate throws. This rewards all-round fielding excellence.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Filters

You can narrow down any leaderboard to find the most relevant comparison for you:

- **Global vs. Regional** — the leaderboard defaults to your region automatically. Tap the filter menu to switch to Global or select a different region manually.
- **This Season vs. All Time** — toggle between your current-season stats and your cumulative all-time record.
- **Friends Only** — restrict the leaderboard to players on your friends list for a personal rivalry view. Great for weekly trash talk.

## Your Position

<Note>
  Your current rank for every category is always pinned to the bottom of each leaderboard panel, even when you sit outside the top 100. You never have to scroll endlessly to find yourself — your card stays visible at all times.
</Note>

Leaderboard standings refresh **every hour**, so the ranks you see are never more than 60 minutes out of date. Check back after a big session to watch your position move in real time.

## Seasonal Rewards

Finishing near the top of the **Ranked Points** leaderboard earns you exclusive cosmetic rewards that carry into the next season:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Top 100 — MVP Nameplate" icon="id-badge">
    The top 100 global Ranked Points leaders at season end receive an exclusive **MVP nameplate** displayed under their in-game name in all multiplayer modes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Top 10 — Legend Bat Trail" icon="sparkles">
    The top 10 finishers earn an animated **Legend bat trail effect** that activates on every swing throughout the following season.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Keep an eye on the Season Timer shown on the Ranked card in the Main Menu. Push hard in the final week of a season — this is when the most movement happens in the top 100 as players grind their last matches.
</Tip>
