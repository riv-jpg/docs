Tokens are the lifeblood of your Stubly Baseball career. Every match you play, every swing you take in the Batting Cages, and every day you log in puts more tokens in your pocket — and you spend them on the bats, gloves, uniforms, and stat upgrades that turn a solid player into a legend. There are no shortcuts and no real-money purchases: every token you own, you earned.

<Note>
  Stubly Baseball contains **no real-money purchases of any kind.** Tokens are earned exclusively through gameplay. You can never buy tokens with real currency, and no pay-to-win mechanics exist in the game.
</Note>

## Earning Tokens

Tokens flow in from nearly everything you do in Stubly Baseball. The more you play — and the better you perform — the faster your balance grows.

### Quick Play

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Match Outcome" icon="trophy" href="/modes/quick-play">
    - **Win:** 80 tokens + 10 tokens per run scored
    - **Loss:** 20 tokens (you always earn something)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Performance Bonuses" icon="star" href="/modes/quick-play">
    - **Home run:** +15 tokens
    - **Pitcher strikeout:** +5 tokens
    - **Highlight catch:** +10 tokens
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Story Mode

Story Mode rewards scale with the stakes. The deeper into the postseason you push, the bigger the payout.

| Milestone | Token Reward |
|---|---|
| Regular Season Win | 40 tokens |
| Playoff Win | 100 tokens |
| Championship Win | 500 tokens |

### Batting Cages

Take on Challenge Mode in the Batting Cages to earn up to **30 tokens per session**. Your payout scales with your performance — nail every timing window to maximize the reward.

### Daily Login Bonus & Weekly Challenges

<Tip>
  Log in every single day to build your streak. The daily login bonus starts at **25 tokens** on day one and climbs all the way to **150 tokens per day** on day 7 and beyond. Missing a day resets your streak, so make it a habit — even a quick login counts.
</Tip>

Weekly challenges add another layer of consistent income. Complete them to earn between **100 and 300 tokens** depending on the challenge tier. Check the Challenges tab on the main menu each week for fresh objectives.

| Source | Token Range |
|---|---|
| Daily Login (Day 1) | 25 tokens |
| Daily Login (Day 7+) | 150 tokens |
| Weekly Challenge (Tier 1) | 100 tokens |
| Weekly Challenge (Tier 3) | 300 tokens |

## Spending Tokens

Put your tokens to work across four categories in the Shop, plus stat upgrades in the Player Editor.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Stat Upgrades" icon="arrow-trend-up" href="/player/upgrading-stats">
    Invest tokens directly into your player's core attributes. See the **Upgrading Stats** guide for the full cost curve across all stat tiers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bats" icon="baseball-bat-ball" href="/shop/bats">
    Unlock bats ranging from **200 to 2,500 tokens** across five rarity tiers — each with unique passive stat bonuses and visual effects.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gloves" icon="hand" href="/shop/gloves">
    Upgrade your fielding with gloves priced from **150 to 2,000 tokens**. Bonuses target Hands, Range, Accuracy, and Arm Strength.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cosmetics" icon="shirt" href="/shop/tokens">
    Customize your look with uniform color unlocks (**100–500 tokens**) and player accessories (**50–300 tokens**) — purely visual, zero stat impact.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Your Token Balance

Keep an eye on your balance so you always know how close you are to the next unlock.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the Top Bar">
    Your current token total is always visible in the **top bar of the main menu** — no need to navigate anywhere.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Your Full History">
    Head to **Settings → Account → Token History** to see a complete log of every token earned and spent, with timestamps and sources.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plan Your Spending">
    Tokens **never expire** and carry over between seasons in Story Mode. There's no rush — save up for the tier of gear that fits your playstyle.
  </Step>
</Steps>
