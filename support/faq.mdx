## Account & Access

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Stubly Baseball free to play?">
    Yes — Stubly Baseball is completely free to download and play. There are no real-money purchases of any kind. Every item, upgrade, and cosmetic in the game is earnable through regular gameplay using tokens.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there crossplay between mobile and PC?">
    Yes — all platforms share the same matchmaking pool. Whether you're on iOS, Android, or PC, you'll be matched with and against players from every platform. Your account and progress are also shared across devices.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I play on multiple devices?">
    Yes. Log into your Stubly account on any supported device and your player, stats, tokens, and progress will all be there waiting for you. Just make sure you're signed in to your account rather than playing as a guest.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I lose access to my account?">
    Tap **Forgot Password** on the login screen to reset your password via email. If your registered email address is no longer accessible, [contact support](/support/contact) directly at account-recovery@stubly.com with proof of ownership, such as your original email address and approximate account creation date.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tokens & Shop

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I earn tokens faster?">
    There are three reliable ways to boost your token income:

    - **Daily login bonus streak** — log in every day to keep your streak alive and collect escalating bonus tokens.
    - **Weekly challenges** — new challenges refresh each week and award tokens on completion.
    - **Quick Play wins** — every win in Quick Play earns tokens, with bonus amounts for win streaks.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do tokens expire?">
    No — tokens never expire. Earn them at your own pace and spend them whenever you're ready. There is no time pressure to use your tokens before they disappear.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I transfer tokens to another player?">
    No. Tokens are account-bound and non-transferable. You cannot send, trade, or gift tokens to other players.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there microtransactions?">
    No. Stubly Baseball has no real-money purchases, no premium currency, and no pay-to-win mechanics. Every piece of content in the shop — gear, cosmetics, bat trails, nameplates — is earnable with tokens from gameplay.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Gameplay

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why do I keep missing pitches?">
    Pitch timing is the most common skill gap for new players. Head to **Batting Cages** from the Main Menu to practice against different pitch types, speeds, and locations without any match pressure. You should also consider upgrading your **Vision** stat in the Player Editor — higher Vision gives you a wider timing window on contact swings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my player's position after creation?">
    Yes — you can change your player's position at any time. Open the **Player Editor** from the Main Menu and navigate to the **Position** tab to switch to any available fielding position. Stats tied to your current position remain intact.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does gear make a big difference in ranked matches?">
    Gear provides meaningful bonuses, but stat bonuses from equipped gear are **capped at +5 per stat** in Ranked matches. This means your base stat upgrades — earned through the Player Editor — matter more at high rank than any gear loadout. Focus on upgrading your core stats consistently as you climb.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I unlock new stadiums?">
    Stadiums unlock automatically as you progress through **Story Mode** seasons. Complete each Story Mode season to add new stadiums to your collection. Once unlocked, every stadium is available to use in Private Matches regardless of your current Story Mode progress.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Online Multiplayer

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why is my rank dropping faster than it goes up?">
    The most common cause is **mid-match disconnections or abandons**. Leaving a Ranked match early — whether by quitting or losing connection — applies a **-30 RP penalty** on top of the standard loss deduction. Finish every match you start to avoid the penalty. If you experience frequent disconnections, see the [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) page for connection fixes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long is a ranked season?">
    A ranked season runs for approximately **8 weeks**. Check the **Season Timer** shown on the Ranked card in the Main Menu for an exact countdown to the current season's end. Season rewards are distributed within 24 hours of the season closing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if my opponent disconnects?">
    After your opponent disconnects, Stubly Baseball opens a **15-second reconnect window** for them to rejoin the match. If they don't reconnect within that window, the match resolves in your favor — you receive the full win, RP gain, and token reward as normal.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
