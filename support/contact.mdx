The Stubly Baseball support team is here whenever you need help. Before reaching out, take a few minutes to check the [FAQ](/support/faq) and [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) pages — the vast majority of common issues are resolved there without any wait time. If those pages don't solve your problem, use one of the contact channels below to get in touch.

<Tip>
  The fastest way to get help is through **in-game support**. Submitting a ticket from inside the game automatically attaches your account details, device info, and recent match history — so the support team has everything they need to help you without extra back-and-forth.
</Tip>

## Contact Channels

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="In-Game Support" icon="gamepad">
    Open **Settings (gear icon) → Help & Support → Contact Us** from anywhere in the game. Your account information is attached to the ticket automatically. This is the recommended method for account issues, missing tokens, and anything match-related.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Support" icon="envelope">
    Send an email to **support@stubly.com** with your username, platform (iOS / Android / PC), and a description of your issue. Include your match ID from **Settings → Match History** if your issue is match-related.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Discord Community" icon="discord">
    Join the Stubly community server at **discord.gg/stubly** and head to the **#support** channel. Community helpers and official Stubly staff are active there around the clock — great for quick questions and real-time help.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Twitter / X" icon="twitter">
    Follow and message **@stubly** on Twitter/X for general feedback, bug reports, and announcements. This channel is best for non-urgent feedback — for account or technical issues, use in-game support or email.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What to Include in Your Request

Providing the right details upfront means faster resolutions. Include the following whenever you contact support:

- **Username** — your exact Stubly in-game username
- **Platform** — iOS, Android, or PC
- **Date and time of the issue** — as specific as possible, including your time zone
- **Match ID** — found in **Settings → Match History** — include this for any issue that occurred during or after a specific match
- **Description of the issue** — what you were doing, what you expected to happen, and what actually happened

## Account Recovery

If you've lost access to your account or believe your account has been compromised, email **account-recovery@stubly.com** directly — do not use the standard support email for this. Include the following proof of ownership in your message:

- The original email address registered to the account
- Your approximate account creation date
- Any other identifying details (username history, purchased items, etc.)

<Note>
  Account recovery requests are handled by a dedicated team and may take longer than standard support tickets. Respond promptly to any follow-up emails they send to speed up the process.
</Note>
