Most issues in Stubly Baseball have a quick fix. Work through the relevant section below to resolve your problem — the steps are ordered from simplest to most involved. If you've tried everything here and the issue persists, head to [Contact Support](/support/contact) and submit a ticket with the details of your problem.

---

## Connection Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't connect to online servers">
    Follow these steps in order until the connection is restored:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check your internet connection">
        Confirm your device has an active internet connection by opening a website or another app. If your connection is down, resolve that first.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Stubly server status">
        Visit **status.stubly.com** to see if there is an active outage or scheduled maintenance. If servers are down, wait for the all-clear before retrying.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart the app">
        Fully close Stubly Baseball and relaunch it. Many connection errors clear on a fresh app start.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check your firewall (PC only)">
        If you're on PC, verify that your firewall or antivirus software isn't blocking Stubly Baseball's network access. Add the game as an exception in your firewall settings if needed.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="High ping / laggy matches">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Switch to a wired connection (PC)">
        Wi-Fi introduces variable latency. Plug directly into your router with an Ethernet cable for the most stable connection.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Close background apps">
        Streaming services, downloads, and other network-heavy apps compete for bandwidth. Close them before starting a session.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select a closer server region">
        Navigate to **Settings → Network → Server Region** and manually select the region geographically nearest to you. Auto-selection occasionally picks a suboptimal region.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Matchmaking takes too long">
    Long queues are usually caused by one of two things:

    - **Off-peak hours** — try playing at a different time of day when more players in your region are active.
    - **High rank tier** — at very high rank, the player pool is smaller, which naturally extends queue times. This is expected behavior. Expanding your match preferences in **Settings → Matchmaking** can help reduce wait times at the cost of slightly wider rank spread.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account and Progress

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Progress not saving">
    Progress only saves to your account when you are **logged in** — not in guest mode. Check the top of the Main Menu to confirm your username is displayed. If you see "Guest," tap it to log in.

    <Note>
      Progress saves automatically after each **completed match**. Force-quitting the app mid-match may cause that match's rewards and stat changes to not be recorded. Always let the post-match screen fully load before closing the app.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lost tokens after a match">
    Token awards are processed within **30 seconds** after the post-match screen appears. If your token total hasn't updated:

    1. Wait up to **5 minutes** — server processing can occasionally be delayed.
    2. Restart the app and check your token balance again.
    3. If tokens are still missing after 10 minutes, [contact support](/support/contact) and include your **match ID**, found in **Settings → Match History**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't log in">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Reset your password">
        Tap **Forgot Password** on the login screen and follow the email instructions to set a new password.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check for account security alerts">
        Look in your registered email inbox for any security notifications from Stubly that may indicate your account was flagged or temporarily locked.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check your linked sign-in provider">
        If you use **Sign in with Google** or **Sign in with Apple**, make sure that Google or Apple account is still active and accessible. A deactivated linked account will prevent login.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Contact support">
        If none of the above steps work, email account-recovery@stubly.com with your username and proof of ownership.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Gameplay and Performance

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Game crashes on launch (mobile)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check OS requirements">
        Confirm your device meets Stubly Baseball's minimum OS version. Outdated operating systems are the most common cause of launch crashes.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Free up storage">
        Stubly Baseball requires at least **500 MB of free storage** to operate. Delete unused apps or media to make space.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Force-close and reopen">
        Swipe the app away completely from your recent apps list, then relaunch.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Reinstall the game">
        If the crash persists, delete and reinstall Stubly Baseball. Your account data is stored server-side and will be fully restored when you log in.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Low FPS / graphics stuttering (PC)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Lower graphics quality">
        Open **Settings → Graphics → Quality** and reduce the preset to Medium or Low. Disable shadows and ambient occlusion first — they have the highest performance impact.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Update your GPU drivers">
        Outdated graphics drivers are a frequent cause of stuttering. Visit your GPU manufacturer's website (NVIDIA, AMD, or Intel) to download the latest drivers.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Close background applications">
        Close browsers, Discord overlays, and any recording or streaming software before launching Stubly Baseball.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Controls not responding">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Controller (PC)">
        Open **Settings → Controls** and confirm your controller is listed as connected and set as the active input device. Disconnect and reconnect the controller if it doesn't appear. Some third-party controllers may require you to enable **Generic Controller Support** in the same menu.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Touch (Mobile)">
        Navigate to **Settings → Controls → Touch Layout** and run the touch calibration tool. This recalibrates hit zones to your specific device's screen dimensions.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Shop and Purchases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Purchased item not showing in inventory">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Wait for server sync">
        Purchased items sync to your inventory within **2 minutes** of purchase. Wait a moment before troubleshooting further.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart the app">
        Close and reopen Stubly Baseball to force a fresh inventory sync with the server.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Contact support">
        If the item is still missing after **10 minutes**, contact support with your username and the name of the item you purchased.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Accidentally purchased the wrong item">
    <Warning>
      Token purchases are **non-refundable**. Please double-check the item details on the confirmation screen before finalizing any purchase. If you believe you purchased an item in error, contact support within **24 hours** of the transaction for a case-by-case review — resolutions cannot be guaranteed but every request is reviewed.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
