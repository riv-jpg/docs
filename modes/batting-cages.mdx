Batting Cages is Stubly Baseball's dedicated solo practice mode — a pressure-free environment where you can refine your swing, learn pitch recognition, and build the muscle memory you need to dominate in Quick Play and Story Mode. It's available offline after your first login, so you can practice anywhere, any time.

<Tip>
  Fire up the Batting Cages for a few minutes before hopping into a Quick Play match. A warm-up session sharpens your reaction time and gets your swing timing dialed in before real tokens are on the line.
</Tip>

## Accessing Batting Cages

Tap the **Batting Cages** card on the main menu to enter. No matchmaking, no waiting — you go straight to the cage setup screen where you choose your sub-mode and configure your session settings.

<Note>
  Batting Cages **does not cost tokens and does not award tokens**. It's purely a skill-building environment. Your in-cage batting average is tracked separately from your competitive stats and does not affect your ranked profile.
</Note>

## Practice Sub-Modes

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Free Swing">
    Free Swing gives you unlimited pitches from an automated pitching machine with full control over every variable. Use it to isolate a specific pitch type or zone that's been giving you trouble.

    **What you can configure in Free Swing:**
    - **Pitch type:** Fastball, Curveball, Slider, Changeup, or Sinker
    - **Speed:** 50–100 mph in 5 mph increments
    - **Strike zone location:** Inner, Outer, High, Low, or Random
    - **Ball speed off bat:** Toggle visual feedback to see exit velocity on every contact
    - **Slow-motion replay on contact:** Toggle to auto-trigger a slow-mo replay each time you make contact — your best tool for refining swing timing

    There are no rounds, no time limits, and no pressure. Swing until you're satisfied, then change the settings and go again.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Challenge Mode">
    Challenge Mode puts you in timed rounds with specific objectives. Hit as many pitches as possible before the clock runs out, or target highlighted strike zones for bonus points. Strong performances earn **XP** that tracks your improvement over time.

    **Challenge types include:**
    - **Hit Count** — connect with as many pitches as possible in 90 seconds
    - **Zone Targeting** — hit pitches into highlighted zones on the field for multiplied points
    - **Power Round** — focus on extra-base hits and home runs within a set number of pitches

    Complete higher-tier challenges to climb your personal Challenge leaderboard and unlock new practice objectives. No tokens are awarded — Batting Cages is a pure skill-building space.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Live Pitcher">
    Live Pitcher replaces the pitching machine with an AI pitcher that throws realistic pitch sequences — mixing location, speed, and pitch type just like a human opponent would. This is the closest simulation of competitive play you can get outside of a real match.

    **Set the difficulty before each session:**

    | Difficulty | Pitch Speed Range | Sequence Complexity |
    |---|---|---|
    | Rookie | 50–70 mph | Simple, predictable patterns |
    | Pro | 70–85 mph | Mixed sequences with occasional off-speed |
    | Legend | 85–100 mph | Full deceptive sequencing, high movement |

    <Tip>
      Train against **Live Pitcher on Legend difficulty** to prepare for high-rank Quick Play opponents. The pitch sequences at Legend tier mirror what elite-ranked players face in online competition.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Session Settings

All three sub-modes share a common settings panel you can adjust at any time during your session.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pitch Speed" icon="gauge" href="/modes/batting-cages#session-settings">
    Set delivery speed from **50 to 100 mph**. Start slow to establish your timing, then increase speed incrementally as you improve.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pitch Type" icon="baseball" href="/modes/batting-cages#session-settings">
    Choose from **Fastball, Curveball, Slider, Changeup,** or **Sinker**. Isolate one pitch type per session for focused improvement.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Strike Zone Location" icon="crosshairs" href="/modes/batting-cages#session-settings">
    Lock pitches to **Inner, Outer, High,** or **Low** — or set to **Random** for a mixed look that simulates real at-bat variety.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Slow-Motion Replay" icon="film" href="/modes/batting-cages#session-settings">
    Toggle the **slow-motion replay on contact** to review your swing mechanics frame by frame after every hit. Your single most effective tool for timing refinement.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Session Summary

At the end of every Batting Cages session, a detailed summary screen breaks down your performance so you can track improvement over time.

| Stat | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| Balls Faced | Total pitches thrown during the session |
| Contact % | Percentage of swings that made contact |
| Extra-Base Hits | Doubles, triples, and home runs recorded |
| Longest Hit Distance | Peak exit distance in feet |
| Spray Chart | Visual map of where your hits landed on the field |

Review your spray chart regularly — a pattern of weak pull-side grounders or fly-outs to the warning track reveals exactly which pitch types and locations need the most work.
