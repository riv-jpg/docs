Story Mode is Stubly Baseball's single-player narrative campaign — a fully cinematic journey from your first day as a minor league draft pick to the last out of a championship-clinching game. Play entirely offline, make decisions that shape your story, and build a legacy that's uniquely yours across up to five dramatic seasons.

<Note>
  Story Mode progress is saved to your Stubly account. If you play as a **guest**, your progress is lost when you close the app. Create a free account to save your career permanently and pick up exactly where you left off.
</Note>

## Starting Your Career

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Story Mode from the Main Menu">
    Tap the **Story Mode** card on the main menu. If you have an existing career, choose it from the career select screen. To begin fresh, tap **New Career**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose or Create Your Player">
    Select your created player as your protagonist. If you haven't built a player yet, the game directs you to the Player Creator before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch the Draft Day Cutscene">
    Experience your player's **draft day cinematic** — a fully rendered 3D scene showing the moment a minor league team selects you and your journey begins. This is your story's opening chapter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Difficulty">
    Choose your starting difficulty: **Rookie**, **All-Star**, or **Legend**. You can change this setting between seasons, so don't worry about locking yourself in permanently.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Begin Season 1">
    Your first regular season schedule loads. Head into your opening game, manage your player between matchdays, and start building toward the Championship.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Season Structure

<Accordion title="Regular Season — 30 Games">
  Each season opens with a **30-game regular season**. Your created player competes in every game, and your individual performance directly influences your team's standings. Win games, hit for the cycle, throw perfect innings — every strong performance stacks Story XP and earns token rewards.

  Between games, the **dugout hub** gives you three options:
  - **Rest** — recover your player's morale and reduce fatigue
  - **Train** — invest time in a specific skill to earn a small stat boost before the next game
  - **Talk to Teammates** — engage in dialogue scenes that build team chemistry and unlock story beats

  Dialogue choices are permanent. What you say to a slumping teammate or a jealous rival shapes the story outcome and affects your team chemistry stat for the rest of the season.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Playoffs — Best of 5 Series">
  The top teams from the regular season advance to a **best-of-5 playoff series**. Stakes are higher, AI opponents are sharper, and the dramatic moments multiply. Cinematic cutscenes fire at key moments — a walk-off hit, a crucial error, a series-deciding final out.

  Win the series to advance to the Championship. Lose, and your season ends — but your Story XP and tokens carry forward to the next season.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Championship Series">
  Win the playoffs and you face the **Championship Series** — the pinnacle of each season's story. Defeat the opposing team in this climactic showdown to complete the season and unlock the next one. The Championship clincher triggers Stubly Baseball's most elaborate cinematic sequence, built specifically for this moment.

  Completing a Championship unlocks the next season, new storylines, an escalating rival, and the season's exclusive cosmetic reward.
</Accordion>

## Progression and XP

Story Mode runs its own progression track that stacks on top of your token-based upgrades from the shop.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Story XP" icon="star">
    Earn **Story XP** through wins and strong personal performances. Story XP levels up your player's base stats directly — separate from shop upgrades, and both systems stack together.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unlocking New Seasons" icon="lock-open">
    Seasons 2 through 5 unlock sequentially. Complete each season's Championship run to open the next chapter of your career and face a more powerful version of your rival.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Token Rewards

Story Mode is one of the most rewarding token sources in the game — especially deep into the playoffs.

| Achievement | Token Reward |
|---|---|
| Win a regular season game | 40 tokens |
| Win a playoff game | 100 tokens |
| Win the Championship | 500 tokens + exclusive Championship Trophy cosmetic |
| Home run (any game) | Bonus tokens (stacks on top) |
| Perfect inning (as pitcher) | Bonus tokens (stacks on top) |

<Tip>
  Push for personal performance bonuses on top of every win. A Championship run where you hit home runs and throw perfect innings can pay out far more than the base reward — and the exclusive cosmetics you earn can't be purchased in the shop at any price.
</Tip>

## Story Mode Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Cinematic Cutscenes" icon="clapperboard">
    Key career moments — draft day, your first home run, playoff clinchers, and the Championship — trigger fully rendered 3D cinematic scenes with dynamic camera work and crowd atmosphere.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rival Player System" icon="user-ninja">
    A rival player from an opposing team emerges at the start of your career and grows in power as you do. Your rival reacts to your success, challenges you in big moments, and becomes the defining antagonist of your five-season story.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team Chemistry" icon="people-group">
    Talk to teammates between games to raise team chemistry. Higher chemistry boosts your AI teammates' performance on the field — neglect your relationships and your team plays worse when you need them most.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Difficulty Settings" icon="sliders">
    Play on **Rookie**, **All-Star**, or **Legend** difficulty. Switch the setting between seasons to challenge yourself as your skills improve — or dial it back if a season feels overwhelming.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Quitting the app mid-game during Story Mode does **not** save match progress. Always finish the current game or use the **Pause → Save & Exit** option to return to the dugout hub safely before closing.
</Warning>
