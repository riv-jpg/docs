Quick Play is Stubly Baseball's online multiplayer mode — step onto the field and face a real opponent from anywhere in the world in real-time, cinematic 3D action. Every match is live, every at-bat counts, and every win earns you tokens to spend in the shop.

<Note>
  Quick Play requires a Stubly account and an active internet connection. If your connection drops mid-match, the game attempts to reconnect for up to 30 seconds before awarding the win to your opponent.
</Note>

## Starting a Quick Play Match

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Quick Play from the Main Menu">
    Tap the **Quick Play** card on the main menu. The game checks your connection and loads your roster automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Player and Confirm Your Loadout">
    Choose your active created player. Review your equipped gear — bat skin, glove, cleats, and any stat-boosting accessories — then tap **Confirm Loadout** to lock in your selections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'Find Match'">
    Tap **Find Match** to enter the matchmaking pool. The system pairs you with an opponent of similar rank. Most matches connect within **10–30 seconds**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Pre-Match Lobby">
    A lobby screen displays both players' names, primary positions, and equipped gear. Use this time to study your opponent's setup before the first pitch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Coin Flip — Play Ball">
    A coin flip determines which player bats first. The camera sweeps into the stadium in a cinematic opening shot, and the match begins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Match Format

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Innings" icon="list-ol">
    Standard matches run **9 innings**. Shorten the game to **3, 5, or 7 innings** by adjusting the Quick Game setting in **Settings → Gameplay**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Roster" icon="users">
    Each team fields 9 players. You control your created player at their assigned position — AI teammates fill every other spot automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Your Role" icon="baseball">
    Take direct control during **at-bats** and whenever the ball is hit to your defensive position. The AI handles all other fielding and base-running.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extra Innings" icon="clock">
    If the score is tied after 9 innings, the game continues into extra innings until one team takes the lead at the end of a complete inning.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Scoring and Token Rewards

Every match — win or loss — earns you tokens. Perform well to maximize your payout.

| Outcome / Action | Tokens Awarded |
|---|---|
| Win | 80 tokens + 10 per run scored |
| Loss (participation) | 20 tokens |
| Home run | +15 tokens |
| Strikeout (as pitcher) | +5 tokens |
| Highlight catch | +10 tokens |

<Note>
  The **mercy rule** ends the game automatically if one team leads by 10 or more runs after the 5th inning. Token rewards are calculated on the final score at that point.
</Note>

<Tip>
  **Maximize your tokens every match.** Play your full 9 innings at the standard length rather than shortening games — more innings means more opportunities to rack up performance bonuses. Prioritize going for home runs in high-count situations and use your pitcher strategically to chase strikeouts when you're ahead. Even a loss pays out more when you swing for extra bonuses.
</Tip>

## After the Match

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Rematch" icon="rotate">
    After the final out, both players are prompted to vote for a **rematch**. If both agree within 15 seconds, a new match starts immediately with the same players and settings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Report a Player" icon="flag">
    Tap the **flag icon** on the post-match results screen to report unsportsmanlike conduct. Reports are reviewed by the Stubly moderation team.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
