## Get Up and Running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the game">
    Head to the [Download & Install](/getting-started/download-install) page and grab the latest version for your platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up for a free Stubly account to save progress, compete online, and access the shop. See [Account Setup](/getting-started/account-setup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your player">
    Open the [Player Editor](/player/player-editor) from the main menu to design your athlete — customize their appearance, choose a position, and set your initial loadout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play your first game">
    Select [Quick Play](/modes/quick-play) from the main menu card for an instant online match, or head to the [Batting Cages](/modes/batting-cages) to warm up first.
  </Step>
</Steps>
