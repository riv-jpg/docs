The Player Editor is your home base for building the athlete you want to be. From how you look under the stadium lights to the bat you carry to the plate, every detail is yours to control — and you can revisit it any time before a match.

## Opening the Player Editor

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your Player Card">
    From the main menu, tap your **Player Card** in the upper-left corner. Your current player portrait and overall rating appear here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Editor">
    Tap the **Edit** button (pencil icon) beneath your player portrait to launch the full Player Editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a tab">
    The Editor opens to the **Appearance** tab by default. Use the four tabs at the top — **Appearance**, **Position**, **Gear**, and **Name** — to move between sections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Every change you make saves automatically. When you're finished, tap **Done** in the top-right corner to return to the main menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editor Tabs

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Appearance">
    Shape your player's look from head to toe. The **Appearance** tab is split into three sub-sections: Body, Face, and Uniform.

    ### Body

    <CardGroup cols={3}>
      <Card title="Height" icon="ruler-vertical">
        Drag the slider to set your player's height anywhere from **5'6"** to **6'8"**. Height is purely cosmetic and does not affect stats.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Build" icon="person">
        Choose from **Slim**, **Athletic**, or **Power** builds. Each changes your player's silhouette and idle animations.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Skin Tone" icon="palette">
        Use the full-color palette and brightness slider to dial in your exact skin tone.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    ### Face

    Customize the finer details of your player's face:

    - **Face Shape** — choose from a lineup of presets (round, square, oval, and more)
    - **Eye Color** — pick from the color palette or use the slider for custom shades
    - **Hair Style** — select a cut from the available style roster
    - **Hair Color** — full palette with a separate slider for highlights
    - **Facial Hair** — choose a style (clean shave, stubble, beard, mustache) and set its color independently
    - **Eye Black** — toggle on or off; when enabled, pick a shade from charcoal to jet black

    ### Uniform

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Jersey Number" icon="hashtag">
        Set any number from **00 to 99**. Your number appears on the back of your jersey and on the scoreboard.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Uniform Colors" icon="swatchbook">
        Set a **primary** and **secondary** color. These apply to your jersey, pants piping, and socks.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Cap Style" icon="baseball-cap">
        Choose a cap style from fitted, snapback, or batting helmet only. Color follows your primary uniform color.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Batting Helmet Visor Tint" icon="glasses">
        Pick a visor tint — clear, smoke, amber, or mirror — for your batting helmet.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    ### Accessories

    | Accessory | Options |
    |---|---|
    | Wristband Color | Full color palette |
    | Compression Sleeve | Left arm / Right arm / Both / None |

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Position">
    Your position shapes your role in every game mode. Set it carefully — it also determines which stats receive the highest weight in your overall rating.

    ### Primary Position

    Tap any position on the interactive field diagram to select it as your primary. The nine available positions are:

    <CardGroup cols={3}>
      <Card title="Pitcher" icon="baseball" />
      <Card title="Catcher" icon="shield" />
      <Card title="First Base" icon="1" />
      <Card title="Second Base" icon="2" />
      <Card title="Third Base" icon="3" />
      <Card title="Shortstop" icon="bolt" />
      <Card title="Left Field" icon="arrow-left" />
      <Card title="Center Field" icon="arrow-up" />
      <Card title="Right Field" icon="arrow-right" />
    </CardGroup>

    Once you select a position, a **stat weight preview** appears on the right side of the screen. This preview shows which stats matter most for that position — for example, Pitchers weigh Velocity and Control heavily, while Outfielders prioritize Arm Strength and Range.

    <Note>
      Your primary position determines your starting stat profile when you create a new player. Choose the position you plan to play most before your first save.
    </Note>

    ### Secondary Position

    Tap **Set Secondary Position** below the field diagram to assign a backup position. Your secondary position is used in **Story Mode** when the team needs you to fill in at a different spot on the field.

    <Tip>
      Setting a complementary secondary position — like Shortstop as a backup to Second Base — makes Story Mode roster decisions feel natural and keeps your fielding stats relevant.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Gear">
    Equip the bat and glove you want to bring to the field. All gear you own appears here, and the 3D viewer updates in real time as you make selections.

    ### Bat

    Tap the **Bat** slot to open your bat inventory. Scroll through owned bats and tap any one to preview it on your player. New players start with the **Starter Ash Bat** by default.

    <Note>
      Each bat carries stat bonuses (Contact, Power, or both). The bonuses are listed on the bat's detail card. See [the Shop section](/shop) for available bats.
    </Note>

    ### Glove

    Tap the **Glove** slot to open your glove inventory and equip an active glove. Glove type automatically adapts to your primary position — pitcher's mitts, catcher's mitts, and fielding gloves are all stored together but filtered to show compatible options first.

    ### 3D Viewer

    The real-time 3D viewer occupies the center of the Gear tab. Drag to rotate your player and inspect how every piece of equipment looks from any angle. Tap the **Full Screen** icon to expand the viewer.

    ### Shop Shortcut

    <Card title="Browse Gear Without Leaving the Editor" icon="shop" href="/shop">
      Tap the **Shop** button in the top-right corner of the Gear tab to jump directly to the gear section of the in-game Shop. Your editor session is saved automatically when you leave and restored when you return.
    </Card>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Name">
    Set how your identity appears everywhere in the game — on your jersey, above your head in multiplayer lobbies, and in the scoreboard.

    ### Player Name

    Tap the **Player Name** field and enter your in-game name. This name:

    - Prints on the **back of your jersey** (truncated to 14 characters)
    - Appears **above your character** in multiplayer matches
    - Shows in **post-game stat screens** and replays

    ### Nickname / Callsign

    Tap the **Nickname** field to add a callsign. Your callsign:

    - Appears in the **scoreboard** during multiplayer
    - Displays in **batting order cards** during Story Mode
    - Can include numbers and standard punctuation (no special symbols)

    <Tip>
      Keep your callsign short and punchy — the scoreboard truncates anything over 10 characters.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
